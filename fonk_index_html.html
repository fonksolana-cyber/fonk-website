About FONK

FONK is created to support phonk and funk creators through a transparent system that uses creator fees as direct rewards. This token turns community support into real value, empowering artists and strengthening the connection between music and its listeners. FONK stands for fairness, recognition, and genuine appreciation.

How It Works

  • Creator fees are distributed directly to artists as transparent rewards.
  • The community participates in decision-making through token-based involvement.
  • Built to empower creators and unify fans in a fair music ecosystem.
